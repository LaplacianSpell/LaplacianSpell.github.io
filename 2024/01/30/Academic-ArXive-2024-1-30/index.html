Recent Idea

今天重读 Weinberg 的引力书的时候读到了有关引力历史的一点东西，猜测水星进动的非牛顿力学是由于“黄道光”这样的分布在太阳系黄道上的物质晕导致的，让我想起了星系的旋转曲线，启发人们对于暗物质的认识。不知道当时的人们是不是这样子受到启发的呢？

关于阅读论文，需要明确自己的目的是什么，比如我现在的目的只有两个，第一是弄明白问题是什么，第二是弄明白他多大程度上怎么解决这个问题的，这种解决方案有没有一些限制。

突然想起上学期在问 X 老师要调研的文献的时候她给了我两篇原始文献，然后自己便开始啃这个原始文献，啃了半天发现并不好读且没消化啥知识，进度很慢并令人沮丧。最后自己突然开窍了决定找一些其他的文章比如综述之类的，于是就完美的水掉了。这件事情给我的启发不在于说我要违背老师的建议种种，而在于说老师的建议不一定可行，或者说不一定适合你。这也启发了在读研究生的过程中，老师的建议不一定是有效的，有两个原因，第一个是老师和自己实际上面对的都是未知的，尚未解决的问题；第二个是老师不一定完全清楚你所处的状态。所以发挥自己的能动性还是挺重要的。

Motivation

Problem Background

The Puzzle: the degeneracy of ground state for quantum system described by the geometry of extremal black hole.(Extremal RN, Kerr and KN) And the degeneracy should be lifted（为什么需要移除简并，这个简并的问题在什么地方，和对称性之间的关系是什么，我现在的理解是现在从引力这个角度去计算一些问题，然后对可能的量子系统加以限制）

  1. Extremal BHs $\rightarrow$ deg Killing horizon $\rightarrow$ vanishing surface gravity $\rightarrow$ non-vanishing horizon area (System with macroscopic ground state degeneracy)解释该种物理系统宏观基态熵的微观起源
  2. Kerr KN are special since there isn’t spherical symmetry in these 2 system.
  3. Several ideas for arguing the vanishing: BH pair production, attractor mechanism in string theory.一些解释微观起源

The persist puzzle at low $T$: $E-E_0 =T^2/M_{gap}$即使在近极限的情况下也有问题

Claim: A mass gap of order $M_{gap}$ in BH’s micro-spectrum

Claim: A near-extremal BH (temperature below the gap, in canonical ensemble) $\rightarrow$ No Hawking radiation [6]

Several recent works solve the problem.（说实话这个 gap 和低温行为的关系我还不是很理解，为什么这么猜测）

Recently Proposed Solution

Idea: Using gravitational path intergral, in the absence of underlying symmentry, deg removed.

Fact: extremal limit BH $\rightarrow$ Near horizon region include $\mathrm{AdS_2}$ and emerge $\mathrm{SL}(2,\mathbb{R})$ symmetry. $\rightarrow$ with set of diffeomorphism modes (zero modes)/Gauge trans. $\rightarrow$ strongly coupled, quantum mechanically treated needed（这里的关系是怎么样的，不是很理解，这些mode怎么来的，为什么又是强耦合的，强耦合又和量子有啥关系）

The method to treat these modes [12], The result is $Z_{\beta}\sim T^{3/2}e^{S_0}$

  • Former derivation to this result of partition function on BHs: BTZ, RN (All spherical)
  • Method: Dimensional reduction of near-horizon region to JT-Gravity

All derivations by using JT can be understood in gravitational path integral framework（这昭示了什么？）

The 2-D perspects

Why Test in Kerr

  1. Formation discussions on the method are mainly based on situation with spherical symmetry. And all recent calculation using gravitational path integral are all in spherical case (RN)
  2. Demensional reduction of NH region of JT Gravity cause some complications:
    • $\mathrm{AdS}_2$ is warped and fibered over compact base space $\rightarrow$ Dimensional reduction to $\mathrm{AdS}_2$ not natural （什么是自然的，这种做法有什么问题）
    • Low temperature corrections to near horizon region are complicated to describe.（对JT来说，困难在什么地方？）
  3. A test of Kerr/CFT: Calculate from both the CFT and Near extremal BH sides.

Former Study of Quantum Corrections to Extremal Kerr Gravitational Path Integral

  • [11] Sen 1109.3706: for extremal kerr, no matter d.o.f, one loop determinant [3] of gravitons around saddle point. $\rightarrow$$\delta S = 64/180\log(A/(4G_N))$
    • classical correction to above $\sim T$ （什么样的修正是经典的？我觉得我的问题在引力的路径积分没学好）
    • quantum, the zero modes contribute to one loop determinant $\rightarrow$ IR divergence $\rightarrow$ regulate with a small finite temperature
  • Former study of zero modes (comes from metric perturbation in 4-d NHEK):
    • Origin: Large diffeomorphisms of near-horizon extremal Kerr region(near-$\mathrm{AdS}_2$ asymptotics)
    • Classification:
      • Tensor zero modes(Schwarzian modes): Schwarzian dynamics of $\mathrm{AdS}_2 $ asymptopia/gravitational fluctuations in $\mathrm{AdS}_2$
      • Vector zero modes(Rotational modes): fluctuation of angular velocity, zero modes of U(1) field (Comes from 4-d metric obtained in the reduction)
    • Calculation:
      • Method: eigenspace of spin-2 fluctuation operator in NHEK with harmonic gauge.
      • Shortcoming: only able to indentify Schwarzian zero modes; rotational zero mode to be singular.
      • Result: in canonical ensemble ($J$ fixed, since $dM = TdS+\Omega dJ$, then we are in canonical ensemble), $\delta S_{quatum} \sim \log T$
      • Subtlties: the sensitivity of ensemble, further calculation can incorporate light matter fields

Method

Extremal Kerr Thermaldynamics

NHEK metric:

$$
ds_0^2 = (1+\cos^2\theta)J^2[(y^2-1)d\tau^2+\frac{dy^2}{y^2-1}+d\theta^2] + \frac{4J^2\sin^2\theta}{1+\cos^2\theta}(d\phi+i(y-1)d\tau)^2
$$

The Quantum effect in extremal Kerr

  • General idea: compute 1-loop determinant of graviton (spin-2 field) around bg geometry.

  • Former Tries’ Technique (Details contained in [11] above):

    • Expand metric fluctuation in orthonormal basis of normalizable modes $f^{(i)}_n(x)$.
    • Define heat kernel: $K^{ij}(x,x’;s)=\sum_ne^{-\lambda_ns}f^{(i)}_n(x)\overline{f}^{(j)}_n(x’)$, the basis diagonalize the kinetic operator with eigenvalues $\lambda_n$.
    • One loop correction of all fluctuations: $\log Z_{\mathrm{1-loop}}=\frac{1}{2}\int_{\Lambda_{\mathrm{UV}}}^{\infty}\frac{\mathrm{d}s}{s}\int\mathrm{d}^4x\sqrt{g}K(x,x;s)$. Where $K$ is $K^{ij}g_{ij}$, contracted with reduced metric in near-horizon spacetime.（不是很理解这里的计算逻辑，看上去像是前面热核展开的某种技巧）
    • Expand heat kernel with $\sqrt{s}$ around $0$. for $d=4$ case, there exist constant term $K_0(x)=K_0$, which grows logarithmically with $A$ after integration.（原文章中的积分区域不是很理解，那个J是怎么进来的）

    For NHEK, it’s easy to compute $K_0$ with or without matter field.

    • Notation: $n_F$ (number of massless Dirac fields), $n_S$(massless scaler fields), $n_V$(massless vector fields)

    • Result: put a correction for the factor before $\log A$

      $$
      \log Z’_{\mathrm{1-loop}} = \frac{1}{180}(2n_S-26n_V+7n_F+424)\log S_0+\dots
      $$

      the gravitons part gives a factor $424/180$, so we should account for the zero modes.(是因为这里看上去没法使用微扰论吗？)

    Here, we circumvent the explicit analysis of eigenspectrum by using the leading large charge correction to the entropy can be extracted from the conformal anomaly.(因此在[11]里面 Sen 并没有分析这些 modes)这里这两篇文章没怎么说清楚，没有分析清楚的原因是什么，为什么需要对零模单独考虑等

  • New Efforts: take zero modes into accounts

    • Method: Construct the quadratic fluctuation operator for the Einstein-Hilbert Theory.
    • Technique: Include a gauge fixing term and expand to Gaussian order. (需要学习一个这里的 gauge 项是怎么来的)
    • Result: $\Delta_Lh_{\mu\nu}=-\nabla_\rho\nabla^\rho h_{\mu\nu}-2R_{\mu\rho\nu\sigma}h^{\rho\sigma}$(我们展开到gauss项得到的结果，现在为了计算高斯项的路径积分，需要得到算符的det，也就是计算特征值)

  • Schwarzian zero modes:

    • Technique:
      • set a perturbation with ansatz: $ds^2_{\mathrm{Sch}}=J^2(1+\cos^2\theta)[H_{\tau\tau}d\tau^2+2H_{\tau y}(\tau,y)d\tau dy + H_{yy}(\tau,y)dy^2]$, pluging the zero modes equation $\rightarrow$ fixed the Hs with some parameter free (can be determinded with assumption that metric is real, normalization can be determined by demanding othgn of eigenfunctions)
      • OR: pick an ansatz for vector field generate the diffeomorphisms $\zeta_T = e^{inT}( f_1(y)\partial_{y}+f_2(y)\partial_t +f_{3}(y)\partial_\varphi) \rightarrow$ get bunch of ODEs
    • Properties of this fields:

      • Non-normalizable, act non-trivially on the boundary:

        $$
        y\rightarrow\infty, \zeta_T \sim -\xi(\tau)\partial_\tau+\xi’(\tau)y\partial_y
        $$

        mode act as time reparametrizations on boundary. so the zero mode matric space is $\mathrm{Diff(S^1)/PSL}(2,\mathbb{R})$, the modulus is isometry of $\mathrm{AdS}_2$, the reparametrization related by isometry are same

      • The diffeomorphisms are determined by their behavior near conformal boundary.

      • the perturbation/vector ansatz pick a way to propagate boundary into bulk. $\iff$ Choose a gauge fixing term to derive Operator.(这一段的道理完全不太明白，为什么由边界决定？根据 Andy 所说，这些零模是由于我的 gauge fixing term 余下的没被 fix 的自由度造成的)

  • Rotational zero modes:A puzzle

    • The first motive: dimensionally reducing to 2 dimensions
      • Gauge vector field admit a set of zero modes associated with gauge transform $A\rightarrow A+dH$
      • $H$ determined by boundary and gauge fixing term $\rightarrow$ Choose Lorenz gauge for $U(1)$
      • the zero mode matric space depend on ensemble: (Grand canonical)Fix $\Omega\rightarrow \mathcal{M}_{\mathrm{U(1)}}=\mathrm{Loop(U(1))/U(1)}$
    • The second motive: straight in NHEK
      • Find metric perturbation correspond to gauge transformation in reduction theory: $d\phi \rightarrow d\phi+dH \iff \xi_R=H(y,\tau)\partial_\phi$
      • Caveat: Not zero mode in NHEK, Fail to satisfy harmonic gauge condition
      • Modify: Add new term $\xi_{R,H} = \xi_R+\xi_H$, claim same boundary behaviour with $\xi_R$
      • Another Caveat: new field is regular at $\theta=0,\pi$

    IMPASSE:

    • general zero mode solution (See Schwarzian, the $\zeta_T$ ansatz include $\xi_R$) seems not include rotation zero mode. Also unable to prove no such modes. Also near-extremal BTZ the same result. may be consistent with dual CFT.（检验了吗，有这种结果吗？？）
    • There still evidence: putative zero modes derived above, the 2-d theory with $U(1)$ Lorenz gauge to argue for uplift Lorenz to 4d metric to get zero modes with new gauge fixing(Break covariance since Schwarzian have different gauge fixing).(或者假设存在sigular是物理的，或者对于不同的mode取不同的gauge，Sen使用2-d下reduce计算考虑了旋转mode，得到考不考虑mode只会改变log的系数）
    • In spherical result: we recover the expected zero modes associated with isomotries of sphere.
    • In rotating BTZ: also no sign of rotational zero modes, consist with dual CFT.
      but in BTZ case the chemical potential is fronzen $\rightarrow$ natural to be no zero mode
    • In 5-d BMPV geometry(similar to BTZ): Only include all near-horizon 7 zero modes does we match with the microscopic calculation.在BMPV也有旋转mode的问题(要么解释背景上的这个Mawell带来的zero modes是我们的转动mode，要么找到一些其他的mode来解释微观计算得到的log结果前面的一些系数).In former analysis. Sen find another mode and assume it to be zero mode.

  • Include zero mode: A practice

Conclusion

The 1-loop order correction, give a more general result: $Z\sim T^{3/2}e^{S_0+c\log S_0}$.

  1. Valid between $\sim e^{S_0}<T<T_q$ left means not to let the saddle point result to be too small (如果太小要考虑其他的鞍点)
