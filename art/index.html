Learning skills

A short summary for the learning skills

Study strategies

  • Retrieval Practices: which works by actively recalling matters and putting them on the paper (when it is hard for you to recall some parts. You know where to review/practice more then.)

  • Interleaving: which works by practicting one of your skills in a period of time then switch to another ,and perhaps another… and switch back to the first (which forms a circle…).

  • Spaced Repeted Practices: based on the Ebbinghaus Forget Curve which tells us that the best time to review the knowledge is when you start to forget it.

How to create a study schedule

  • Double the time estimates: when you want to estimate the time you will use to do something.You’d better double the estimated time, which is usually the true time to finish the tasks.

  • Make 5 Days Study Plan!: Break the material to work into several chunks.Plan to spend 2hrs each day to prepare for one of the chunks(By means of what we talked below [HOW TO PREPARE FOR THE EXAM])

How to prepare for the exam

  • Preparing the materials: this is an active action not a passive process, in which you organize, summarize and consolidate all the stuffs you learned, instead of just re-write your notes or do something like that.You can put yourself as a lecturer for a study group and then preparing lecturenotes for them.

  • Review the materials: Try to explain the lecturenotes to yourself or the group members.

SQ3R Textbook Reading System

  • SURVEY: Glance the head (or summary charpter) to grasp the main ideas of each sections/chapter and the way to organize them.
  • Question: Turn the head into a QUESTION and bring it with you when you read the stuffs below
  • READ: Read to EXPLORE the answer(Which means you do not just plod passively but do the research actively)
  • RECITE: Leave the book and recite the answer in your own words (Remark: Maybe I can combine the step with the Cornell-notetaking system)
  • REVIEW: Summary the main points and there relations, recall the subpoints.

制冷剂

“到点就收场，不管做成啥样都交上去，不要为了眼前短期的收获而牺牲完成下一个任务的能力，不要指望着没睡够的时候的脑子能“强迫自己”做出理性决策。真正有用的是对体力/精力/注意力资源进行合理分配，该充电的时候放下一切充电，反正一觉醒来可能问题已经在脑子后台自动解决了，既不需要牺牲娱乐时间也不降低效率”

“物理方向的收益比起其他方向要差的多，很有可能其他人都不认可你的理论，所以我们自己更要 enjoy it。如果自己都不能从中得到满足和快乐，为什么还要选这样一条性价比低的路呢？”

“按照自己的节奏一点点打好基础，做一个东西不管能不能做出来，都要把那一片东西弄懂，最后就能慢慢走出自己的一片世界。”
